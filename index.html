Brian Ho

I do computer things.

If you haven't already assumed from the title page text or the big yellow font in front of the wall of penguin GIFs that I designed myself, my name is Brian Ho (wow!). I got my Bachelor's at UC Berkeley, studying Computer Science with a minor in Music. I am eager to pursue a goal that involves critical thinking, and challenging my knowledge of the CS fundamentals as well as my programming skills.


Wow Brian, you're so boring and pretty much a nerd. Do you even do anything for fun?

Funny you should ask! Aside from playing PC games, watching shows (both animated and live action), magic tricks, and drawing, I am involved in the University of California Marching Band (Cal Band Great!). I play the mellophone and have been on various committees of structure within the band - in 2017, I was the mello Director's Assistant (imagine it as section leader) and in 2018, I was part of the Musical Activities Committee, which is the committee that is in charge of all things musical - from conducting at various venues (such as volleyball games or birthday parties) to arranging some of the music we perform in shows.

I also like eating at Chick-Fil-A for fun.
